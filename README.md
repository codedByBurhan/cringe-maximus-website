# 🤮 cringe-maximus-website

### The World's Worst Landing Page — A Masterpiece of Suffering

🌐 **Live Site:** [https://codedByBurhan.github.io/cringe-maximus-website/](https://codedByBurhan.github.io/cringe-maximus-website/)

> Inspired by the golden age of GeoCities circa 2003. This page was built with zero regard for the user, maximum disregard for design principles, and an unwavering commitment to being absolutely terrible.

---

## 💀 Features

### Visual Assault
- Neon green tiled background with hot pink diagonal stripes — visible from space
- Clashing color scheme of `#FF00FF`, `#00FF00`, `#FF6600`, `#0000FF` used simultaneously and aggressively
- Blinking `<h1>` title with competing red and blue text shadows
- Giant "WOW!" rendered in 72px Impact font for absolutely no reason
- A decorative "Hello World" where every single letter is a different color, size, and font
- Rotating and skewed `<div>` elements scattered throughout
- Crosshair cursor sitewide

### Popup Hell
- **Popup #1** fires on page load: "YOU ARE OUR 1,000,000TH VISITOR — YOU WIN A FREE iPOD NANO!!!"
- **Popup #2** triggers on hover AND click of the main CTA button — just when you think you're safe
- **Exit intent warning** via `window.onbeforeunload` guilt-trips you for trying to leave
- All popups are rotated at an angle for extra instability

### Broken Navigation
- Nav bar built with Windows 98-era `outset` borders
- Link #3 has a random `margin-left: 80px` gap for no reason
- Link #5 is `position: relative` and overlaps other links
- Every link leads nowhere (`href="#"`)
- Includes an "UNDER CONSTRUCTION" link (the page itself is also under construction)

### Typography Crimes
- Comic Sans, Papyrus, Impact, and Courier New all used on the same page
- Font sizes range from `6px` to `72px` within the same section
- White text on yellow background, yellow text on red background, green text on blue background
- ALL CAPS mixed with MiXeD cAsE throughout
- A legal disclaimer in `8px` font about 847-page terms and conditions

### Audio Nightmare
- Fake MIDI autoplay bar proudly announces *"Fur Elise (Best of Best Mix 2003)"* loading at 99%

### Dual Marquees
- Two `<marquee>`-style scrolling banners running simultaneously in **opposite directions**
- One advertises free ringtones and Limewire Pro
- The other warns you have 37 viruses and offers hot singles in your area

### Broken Layout
- Tables nested **3 levels deep** for a simple 2-column display
- A "CLICK ME" button positioned `left: 60%` so it bleeds off-screen
- Right column overlaps the left column via negative `margin-left`
- Right column is also offset 30px downward for maximum misalignment
- Two broken image placeholders (classic missing image icon)

### Infinite Scroll Trap
- After dismissing the first popup, a `setInterval` fires every **1.8 seconds**
- Injects a new rainbow-colored, randomly rotated banner section forever
- Messages include classics like *"CLICK HERE to subscribe to our newsletter (you cannot unsubscribe)"*

### Fake Social Proof
- Visitor counter displaying `004,892,047` in Courier New on a navy background
- Guestbook with signed entries from:
  - `xX_DarkWolf99_Xx`
  - `CoolGirl2003` (includes her MSN Messenger address)
  - `h4x0r_supreme`
  - `WebmasterSteve` (the author)
- Rainbow-cycling `WIN $1,000,000` banner (color cycles every 0.3s)

### Fine Print
- `tiny-text` class set to `6px` used liberally for legal disclaimers
- "*money back not available*" hidden under the guarantee
- "*this claim has not been verified*" under the 2003 founding date
- "*price not guaranteed*" under the sale price
- "*product may not exist*" under the product image
